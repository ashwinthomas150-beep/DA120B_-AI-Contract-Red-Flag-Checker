What we catch

The clauses most people
miss until it's too late

High Risk

Unlimited Non-Compete

Clauses that restrict your ability to work in your field after leaving — often worded far more broadly than is legally enforceable.

High Risk

IP Overreach

Employers claiming ownership of any work you create, even on personal time with your own equipment and no company resources.

High Risk

Mandatory Arbitration

Clauses that force you to settle disputes through private arbitration, waiving your right to sue or join a class action.

Medium Risk

Unilateral Amendment

Provisions that let the other party change contract terms — salary, role, hours — without your explicit consent.

Medium Risk

Vague Termination

At-will or "for cause" clauses defined so loosely that termination can happen without clear grounds or required notice.

Low Risk

Broad Confidentiality

NDA clauses that are unusually wide in scope or duration — not immediately dangerous, but worth knowing before you sign.
